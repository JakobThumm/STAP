Looking at the desktop from behind:
Top port: Ethernet enp5s0 (Internet connection):
	- plug in blue cable and connect it to your laptop. 
	- in your laptop activate wifi forwarding and allow forwarding in through your firewall. Something like this (requires sudo):
```
iptables -I FORWARD -i enp0s31f6 -o wlp0s20f3 -j ACCEPT
iptables -I FORWARD -i wlp0s20f3 -o enp0s31f6 -m state --state RELATED,ESTABLISHED -j ACCEPT
```
	- on this PC, select the ethernet connection "Manual"
